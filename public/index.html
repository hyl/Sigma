What is Σigma?

Σigma (pronounced Sigma) is an online anonymous chat website. You get connected to a random stranger from anywhere in the world and we let you have a one-one chat.

Σigma is free to use, and has no adverts. All server costs are covered by the creator, /u/roomzinchina. However, donations are nice.

Σigma is also open source, and you can find all the code on GitHub. A huge thanks goes out to all the users which have submitted code and helped us along the way. You"re awesome.

Is it anonymous?

Yes, Σigma is totally anonymous. The person you talk with won"t know anything about you or your computer (unless you tell them, which we don"t recommend).

While the person you are talking to does not know anything about you or your computer, we keep a record of all messages for up to one year.

Our logs can contain information including: your IP address, your browser name (Chrome/Firefox/Safari etc), messages and pictures you send, and the number of messages you send.

What are the rules?

We have written the rules to be as simple as possible. Please make sure you read them before starting.

  1. People under the age of 18 must have permission from a legal guardian before accessing/using Σigma
  2. The Internet is a dangerous place. We do not allow users under the age of 13 to use Σigma. No exceptions.
  3. We do not tolerate abusive language. Swearing/cursing is permitted (to an extent), however phrases like go kill yourself will result in immediate bans.
